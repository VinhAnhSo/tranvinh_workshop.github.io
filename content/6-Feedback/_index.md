### General Evaluation

**1. Working environment**  
The working environment at AWS is friendly, open, and comfortable for interns. Everyone is willing to support me whenever I face difficulties, even during self-study and practice outside working hours. The learning and working space is also convenient and helps me stay focused. However, I think adding more networking or team-bonding activities would help members connect better.

**2. Support from mentors / team admin**  
The mentors are very supportive, explain clearly whenever I do not understand something, and always encourage me to ask questions. The team admin also provides helpful support regarding procedures, documents, and necessary information throughout the program. What I appreciate most is that the mentors do not simply give answers, but create opportunities for me to explore, experiment, and learn from experience.

**3. Relevance between internship content and my major**  
The internship content is generally relevant to my academic orientation in Information Technology and Information Security. In addition to reinforcing my foundational knowledge, the program also helps me access many practical topics related to cloud computing, AWS services, systems, and security.

**4. Learning and skill development opportunities**  
During the internship, I had the opportunity to gain new knowledge and skills such as cloud system design thinking, using AWS services, self-learning, teamwork, report writing, and communication in a professional environment. In addition, participating in workshops and technical events also broadened my perspective on technology and career orientation.

**5. Culture and teamwork spirit**  
I experienced a positive environment where people respect one another, work seriously, and remain open and willing to share. Even as an intern, I still felt that I was part of the team and that my opinions were listened to during both learning activities and technical discussions.

**6. Policies and support for interns**  
The program provides good conditions for interns in terms of learning, participating in activities, and accessing technical materials. In addition, being able to attend internal sharing sessions, workshops, and technology events is a major advantage, as it makes the internship practical rather than purely theoretical.

### Additional Questions

- **What I was most satisfied with during the internship:**  
  What I appreciated most was the opportunity to gain real exposure to AWS and cloud technologies in a structured but still supportive and open environment.

- **What I think the program could improve for future interns:**  
  I think the program could add more networking, orientation, or experience-sharing sessions between different cohorts so that interns can connect and learn from one another more effectively.

- **Would I recommend this internship to my friends? Why?**  
  Yes. I would definitely recommend this program because it is a very good environment for learning, gaining practical exposure to cloud technologies, and developing both technical and professional skills.

### Suggestions and Expectations

- It would be helpful to have more networking or knowledge-sharing activities between mentors and interns to strengthen connections within the program.
- More hands-on exercises or small group projects could be added so that interns have more opportunities to apply what they learn in practice.
- I would be happy to continue participating in similar programs in the future in order to deepen my knowledge and further develop my professional skills.
- **Other comments:**  
  Overall, I consider this internship program to be highly valuable, as it helped me gain practical knowledge and better define my future career direction.
