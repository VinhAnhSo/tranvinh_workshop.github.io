### Week 6 Achievements:
- Developed a solid understanding of AWS SQS and SNS, including how queue-based messaging and publish/subscribe communication are used in distributed systems.
- Learned the differences between SQS and SNS and identified suitable use cases for each service in asynchronous application integration.
- Explored EventBridge and understood how it can be used to route events between AWS services and custom application components in a flexible and scalable way.
- Gained knowledge of Step Functions, including how workflows are modeled using state machines to coordinate multiple tasks and services.
- Practiced designing simple workflows and event-routing patterns to improve understanding of orchestration and service-to-service communication.
- Integrated SQS, SNS, EventBridge, and Step Functions in a basic event-driven architecture to observe how these services work together in real scenarios.
- Improved practical skills in building loosely coupled systems where components communicate through events and messages rather than direct synchronous calls.
- Strengthened overall understanding of application integration patterns on AWS, especially in the areas of asynchronous processing, workflow automation, and event-driven system design.
