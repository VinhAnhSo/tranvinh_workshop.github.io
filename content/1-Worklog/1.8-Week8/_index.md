### Week 8 Achievements:
- Developed a solid understanding of AWS monitoring and observability services, especially the different roles of CloudWatch, CloudTrail, and X-Ray in maintaining system visibility.
- Learned how CloudWatch metrics and alarms can be used to track resource usage, monitor application health, and notify administrators when abnormal behavior or threshold violations occur.
- Practiced working with CloudWatch monitoring data, which improved understanding of how to observe performance trends and respond to operational issues more effectively.
- Explored CloudTrail and understood its importance in audit logging, account activity tracking, and reviewing actions performed within an AWS environment.
- Gained practical knowledge of how CloudTrail can help improve governance, security monitoring, and accountability by recording management and service events.
- Studied AWS X-Ray and learned how distributed tracing can be used to analyze request flows, measure service latency, and identify bottlenecks across multiple application components.
- Improved understanding of how monitoring, logging, and tracing work together to provide a more complete operational picture of cloud-based systems.
- Successfully built a basic comprehensive monitoring setup that combined metrics, alarms, audit logs, and tracing features to strengthen system reliability and troubleshooting capability.
- Strengthened overall awareness of operational best practices in AWS, especially in the areas of observability, incident detection, performance analysis, and system diagnostics.
